GCD

Given two integers a and b, print the GCD of the smallest and the largest perfect square in that range (both a and b inclusive).



INPUT

If no perfect square exists in that range, print -1.A single line containing two space-separated integers a and b.

OUTPUT

A single integer, i.e., the GCD of the smallest and the largest perfect square in that range, or -1.

CONSTRAINTS


2 <= a < b <= 100000

SAMPLE TESTS

1:

Input-

2 10

Output-

1
