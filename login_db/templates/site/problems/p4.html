Floating Point

For given n space-separated natural numbers, find the sum of reciprocals of those numbers and then divide by n.



INPUT

First line contains 'n', the number of natural numbers.

OUTPUT

Second line containing n space-separated natural numbers.Single line containing sum of reciprocals divided by n.

CONSTRAINTS


1<=n<=10^5

SAMPLE TESTS

1:

Input-

2

1 2

Output-

1.5
