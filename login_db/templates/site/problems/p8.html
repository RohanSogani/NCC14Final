Problem 2

Given two integers a and b, print the GCD of the smallest and the largest perfect square in that range (both a and b inclusive). If no perfect square exists in that range, print -1.

Input: A single line containing two space-separated integers a and b

Output: A single integer, i.e., the GCD of the smallest and the largest perfect square in that range, or -1.

Constraints:

2 <= a < b <= 100000
