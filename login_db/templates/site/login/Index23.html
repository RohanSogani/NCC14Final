About NCC



National computing contest is an annual computing contest organized by PICT IEEE Student Branch The very first NCC event took place in 2009 with a motive to introduce computational and real life problems in a new and innovative way. NCC consists of problems based on data structures, algorithms and general logic. Every year, a new and interesting format is created for the contest. All the funds that are collected through NCC are donated to Nalini Foundation to support their cause.
