NATIONAL COMPUTING CONTEST



THE CONTEST BEGINS IN



How we started?

NCC is an event intiated under PICT IEEE Student Branch, started by programmers, for programmers. It's an annual programming competition for everyone to test their skills and be a part of a challenge that is one of a kind. NCC was conceived with only one goal in mind, that is to find the programmer in you.


NCC'14

A series of challenging coding problems to test not only your coding skills but also your logical approach towards them. The contest spans for 4 hours and 4 levels. You advance to next level by clearing the minimal threshold of the current level.

The winner is the contestant who crosses the maximum number of levels. A tie on levels is broken by a contestant's cummulative score. The contestant with the maximum score among these wins. If this too leads to a tie, then the contestants' cummulative submission time will be taken into account.

Get ready for an engrossing duel!
