How we started?

NCC is an event initiated under PICT IEEE Student Branch; started by programmers, for programmers. It's an annual programming competition for everyone to test their skills and be a part of a challenge that is one of a kind. NCC was conceived with only one goal in mind, that is to find the programmer in you.

NCC'14

A series of challenging coding problems to test your not only your coding skills but also your logical approach towards them. The contest spans for 4 hrs and 4 levels. You advance to next level by clearing the minimal threashold of the current level.

The winner is the costent who crossed the max number of levels. A tie on levels is broken by a contestant's cummulative score. The contestant with the maximum score among these wins. If this too leads to a tie; then the contestants' cummulative submission time will be taken into account.

Get ready for an engrossing duel!
